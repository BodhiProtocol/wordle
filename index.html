About Wordle

The Origin

Created by Josh Wardle in 2021 as a gift for his partner. After going viral, The New York Times bought it in 2022 for over $1 million. It now has millions of daily players worldwide.

How to Play

Guess the hidden word. After each guess, tiles show how close you are — 🟩 right letter right spot, 🟨 right letter wrong spot, ⬜ letter not in the word. Kids mode: 3-letter words, 8 tries, 1 hint. Adult mode: 5-letter words, 6 tries, no hints.

Strategy Tips

  • Start with a word that uses common letters: CRANE or SLATE
  • Use yellow letters in a different position next guess
  • Eliminate vowels early — most words have 1-2
